The App

BUILT FOR
YOUR CAMPUS

NaviX turns any campus into a navigable, intelligent space. Scan a QR code at the entrance and instantly get step-by-step guidance to any building, lab, or facility — even offline.

Designed for students, accessible for everyone. Built with React Native, Expo, and a Three.js AR engine.

What's Inside

SIX REASONS
TO USE NAVIX

Every feature engineered to solve a real problem students face on campus, every day.

01
AR Navigation
Real-world 3D arrows rendered over your camera view using Three.js + OpenGL and your phone's magnetometer.
02
QR Location Anchors
Physical QR codes placed around campus instantly anchor your position — no GPS, no guessing.
03
Voice Guidance
Hands-free spoken turn-by-turn instructions. Walk confidently without staring at your screen.
04
Fully Offline
The entire campus map lives on your device. No Wi-Fi, no data — NaviX works anywhere on campus.
05
Accessible Routing
One toggle switches to accessible-only paths — no stairs, no barriers. Inclusive navigation for everyone.
06
Smart Pathfinding
Dijkstra's algorithm computes the optimal route between any two nodes in milliseconds.
